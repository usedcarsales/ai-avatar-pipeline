## 1. Character Archetype Research

### The Highest-Performing AI Avatar Personality Types

Based on analysis of successful virtual influencers (Lil Miquela, Imma, Shudu, corporate AI avatars) and platform performance data:

#### Archetype 1: The Professional Expert
**Profile:** Subject matter expert, credible, calm, authoritative  
**Style:** Business casual or professional attire, clean background, direct eye contact  
**Voice:** Measured pace, clear diction, authoritative tone  
**Best for:** B2B marketing, corporate training, financial/legal/medical content  
**Engagement driver:** Trust and credibility  
**Example use:** "3 things SMBs get wrong about AI automation"

#### Archetype 2: The Friendly Guide
**Profile:** Warm, approachable, conversational — feels like a knowledgeable friend  
**Style:** Casual attire, natural backgrounds, slight smile  
**Voice:** Conversational pace, emotive, personal  
**Best for:** E-learning, customer service, product explainers, lifestyle content  
**Engagement driver:** Relatability and likability  
**Example use:** "Let me walk you through how this works..."

#### Archetype 3: The Brand Ambassador
**Profile:** High polish, aspirational, brand-aligned aesthetic  
**Style:** On-brand attire/colors, premium backgrounds, posed look  
**Voice:** Polished, enthusiastic, brand-voice consistent  
**Best for:** Product launches, social media brand content, campaign-specific  
**Engagement driver:** Aspiration and brand association  
**Example use:** Product demo, brand announcement

#### Archetype 4: The UGC Creator
**Profile:** Casual, authentic-looking, user-generated-content aesthetic  
**Style:** Casual setting (desk, living room), natural lighting, less polished look  
**Voice:** Casual, uses slang appropriately, genuine emotion  
**Best for:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, younger audiences, product reviews  
**Engagement driver:** Authenticity and trust ("this person is like me")  
**Example use:** "I tested this AI tool for 30 days — here's what happened"

## 2. Visual Style Recommendations

### Color & Background
- **Corporate/B2B:** Clean white, gray, or brand-colored backgrounds. Minimal distractions.
- **Social/consumer:** Lifestyle settings, natural environments, on-location looks (even if AI-generated)
- **L&D/Training:** Neutral backgrounds with on-screen text/graphics. Avatar as presenter, not focal point.

### Avatar Appearance
- **Diversity:** Deploy multiple avatars to reach different demographics. Don't default to a single look.
- **Age match audience:** Younger audiences respond better to younger presenters; B2B professionals to peer-age presenters
- **Cultural localization:** When expanding to non-English markets, use culturally appropriate avatar appearance, not just translated voice

### Voice Characteristics
- **Pacing:** 130-160 words per minute for training content; 150-180 WPM for social content
- **Emotion:** Add subtle emotion markers in Fish Audio (`(excited)`, `(thoughtful)`) to avoid robotic delivery
- **Pauses:** Strategic pauses at key points increase comprehension by 20%+ in training contexts

## 3. Platform-Specific Content Strategy

### YouTube (Long-form)
**Optimal length:** 8-20 minutes  
**Best content types:** Tutorials, deep dives, explainers, case studies  
**Avatar strategy:** Professional Expert archetype; stable professional background  
**Title formula:** "[Number] [Specific Result] Using [Topic]"  
**Posting frequency:** 1-2x per week  
**Thumbnail:** Avatar face + text overlay (AI avatars perform well in thumbnails)

### YouTube Shorts
**Optimal length:** 30-60 seconds  
**Best content types:** Quick tips, myth-busting, hooks for long-form content  
**Avatar strategy:** Any archetype; high energy, fast pace  
**Caption:** Full captions critical (70%+ watch without sound)  
**Posting frequency:** 3-7x per week  
**Hook:** First 3 seconds must grab — use surprising stat or question

### TikTok
**Optimal length:** 15-45 seconds (peak), up to 60 seconds  
**Best content types:** Educational (surprisingly strong), behind-the-scenes, challenges  
**Avatar strategy:** UGC Creator archetype for highest engagement  
**Algorithm priority:** Completion rate is #1 signal — keep under 30 seconds to maximize completion  
**Captions:** Required for accessibility + non-sound viewing  
**Posting frequency:** 1-3x per day for algorithm favor

### Instagram Reels
**Optimal length:** 15-30 seconds  
**Best content types:** Product demos, quick tips, lifestyle  
**Avatar strategy:** Brand Ambassador or Friendly Guide  
**Audio:** Original audio (even if AI voice) can trend if engaging  
**Posting frequency:** 3-5x per week

### LinkedIn
**Optimal length:** 1-3 minutes  
**Best content types:** Industry insights, professional tips, company updates  
**Avatar strategy:** Professional Expert archetype — most important platform for this  
**Format:** Captions always on; text hook in first frame  
**Posting frequency:** 2-3x per week  
**Note:** LinkedIn video gets 5× more engagement than text posts

## 4. Content Formats That Perform with AI Avatars

Ranked by consistency of performance across platforms:

### 1. "Myth-Busting" / Counterintuitive Take
**Format:** "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's why..."  
**Length:** 30-90 seconds  
**Why it works:** Pattern interrupt; creates curiosity; shareable  
**AI avatar advantage:** Professional presenter increases credibility of contrarian claim

### 2. Numbered Lists / Tips
**Format:** "3 ways to [achieve outcome]"  
**Length:** 60-180 seconds  
**Why it works:** Clear value proposition; easy to watch through to end  
**AI avatar advantage:** Can iterate same format with different topics rapidly

### 3. Process Walkthroughs / Explainers
**Format:** "How [topic] works in [X] minutes"  
**Length:** 2-10 minutes  
**Why it works:** Search intent (people Google "how does X work")  
**AI avatar advantage:** Can narrate screen recordings or slides; doesn't need to show face

### 4. Case Studies / Results
**Format:** "[Company/person] did [thing] and got [result]"  
**Length:** 2-5 minutes  
**Why it works:** Social proof + actionable insight  
**AI avatar advantage:** Present client wins without client on camera

### 5. Training / Onboarding Modules
**Format:** "Welcome to [course]. In this module, you'll learn..."  
**Length:** 3-10 minutes  
**Why it works:** B2B demand for scalable training content; clear ROI  
**AI avatar advantage:** Update script without re-recording; multilingual automatically

## 8. Disclosure Strategy

**Legal requirement:** FTC and platform rules increasingly require disclosure of AI-generated content  
**Best practice:** Proactive transparency → builds trust

**Recommended disclosure approaches:**
1. In-video: Brief "AI-generated content" label in first 3 seconds
2. Description: "This content features an AI-generated avatar. Scripts are created by [name]."
3. Caption: "#AIGenerated" hashtag (emerging standard)

**Strategic note:** Early adopters who disclose transparently will build trust advantage as audiences become more AI-content aware. Trying to hide AI origin is both risky legally and increasingly obvious to audiences.
